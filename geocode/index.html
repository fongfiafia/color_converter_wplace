FAQ

How do I find latitude and longitude for a city or place in WPLACE?
Enter a city or place in the input, click Find Coordinates (or press Enter). Select any result to center the WPLACE map and get a shareable wplace.live WPLACE link.
Can I share a direct link to a search?
Yes. After you search, this WPLACE page URL includes q (query) and zoom. Share the WPLACE URL and the page will auto-run that search on open; the wplace.live link will be ready.
Where do the results on WPLACE come from?
Results are powered by OpenStreetMap's Nominatim service and shown in WPLACE. Please follow its usage policy and keep attribution when using WPLACE and sharing wplace.live links.
How accurate are the coordinates in WPLACE?
Accuracy depends on the underlying OpenStreetMap data and the type of place searched (country, city, address, POI). In WPLACE, choose the result that best matches your intent, then copy the wplace.live link.
What zoom levels are supported in WPLACE?
The WPLACE map supports zoom levels 1–19. You can change zoom any time; the wplace.live WPLACE link updates automatically.
Does this WPLACE page store my searches?
We do not store your queries on a server. Your selected WPLACE language preference is saved locally in your browser to improve your wplace.live experience.
